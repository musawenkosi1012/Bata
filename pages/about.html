About Bata Zimbabwe

Over 130 years of quality footwear and trusted craftsmanship in Zimbabwe

Our Heritage in Zimbabwe

Bata has been a trusted name in Zimbabwean footwear for over four decades, serving generations of families with quality shoes that combine durability, comfort, and style. Since our establishment in Zimbabwe, we have been committed to understanding the unique needs of our customers and delivering footwear solutions that enhance their daily lives.

Our journey in Zimbabwe began with a simple mission: to provide affordable, high-quality footwear for every member of the family. Today, we have grown to become one of the most recognized and trusted footwear brands in the country, with stores in major cities and towns across Zimbabwe.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of the Zimbabwean lifestyle and climate. Our shoes are designed to withstand the diverse conditions of our beautiful country, from the bustling streets of Harare to the rural communities where durability and comfort are paramount.
