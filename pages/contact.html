Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

+

Most of our stores are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sunday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. However, hours may vary by location. Please check our Store Locator for specific store hours.

Do you offer shoe repair services?

+

Yes! Selected Bata stores offer professional shoe repair services. This includes sole replacement, heel repair, and general maintenance. Contact your nearest store to confirm availability.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn shoes in original packaging with receipt. Defective products can be returned within 90 days. Please bring your receipt and the original packaging.

Do you offer corporate sales or bulk orders?

+

Yes, we provide corporate sales services for businesses, schools, and organizations. Contact our corporate sales team at corporate@bata.co.zw or call +263 4 123 4569 for special pricing and bulk order arrangements.

How can I find my correct shoe size?

+

We recommend visiting our stores for professional fitting. Our trained staff can measure your feet and recommend the best size and style for your needs. We also have size guides available in-store and online.

Do you have an online ordering system?

+

We're currently developing our online ordering system. For now, you can browse our products online and visit our stores to make purchases. Some locations offer phone orders for pickup.
