Children's Size Guide

Finding the right size for your child is important for comfort and healthy foot development. Use our size guide to ensure the perfect fit.

Size Chart

Fitting Tips

  • Measure your child's feet regularly as they grow quickly
  • Always measure both feet as they may be different sizes
  • Leave about 1cm (thumb width) of space between the longest toe and shoe front
  • Shop for shoes in the afternoon when feet are naturally larger
  • Check that the shoe width accommodates your child's foot comfortably
  • Have your child walk around in the shoes to ensure comfort
